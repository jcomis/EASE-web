Preventative actions and tips

Use your humidifier to prevent mold

Issue
Conditions for mold growth occur when humidity is above 50% and the temperature is warm (above 80Â°F)

Solution
Using a dehumidifier, keep humidity levels at 30% - 50%

Dust using a damp cloth

Issue: Dusting with a feather duster can release dust particles into the air and aggravate allergies

Solution: Wipe off surfaces with a damp cloth instead

Vacuum regularly

Issue: Rugs and carpets trap particles in their fibers and release them into the air as you walk around

Solution: Vacuum regularly and shampoo or steam clean your carpets semi-regularly
