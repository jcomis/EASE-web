Give your device a name that will help you identify it (e.g. Home, Work, Bedroom). This name will be used when you receive alerts about the air quality around this device.

EASE can provice you with suggestions for improving your air quality when it reaches levels that cause you problems. It needs to konw what actions you are capable of doing near the device.
